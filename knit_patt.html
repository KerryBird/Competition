What Should I Make?



  • There are hundreds of great patterns that are freely available online, with detailed instructions and pictures to help you out! Below are some great sites to check out

    1. Ravelry.com

     2. Knitty.com

     3. knittingpatterncentral.com

     That should get you started! It's a good idea to start with a basic square, to let you practise casting on and off, and basic knit and purl stitches. Once you've got that down, scarves and fingerless gloves are especially easy to knit, making them great first projects!
