Knitting Terminology

What Do Those Abbreviations Mean?



  • CO = Cast On
  • BO = Bind Off
  • K2tog = Knit 2 Together
  • P2tog = Purl 2 Together
  • SSK = Slip, Slip, Knit
  • M1 = Make 1 Stitch
  • MC = Main colour
  • CC = Contrasting color
  • Dec = Decrease
  • Sl st = Slip stitch
  • RS = right side of work
  • WS = wrong side of work
